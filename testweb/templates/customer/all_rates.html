Help - Shipment Rates
How to use this page:
  • Select individual shipments or use "Select All" to perform bulk actions
  • Choose a courier from the dropdown and click "Assign to Selected Shipments"
  • To assign a courier to a single shipment, use the dropdown in the "Assign Courier" column
  • Shipment status is shown in the Status column (Assigned/Pending)
Weight Information:
  • Gross Weight: Actual weight of the shipment
  • Volume Weight: Calculated based on dimensions
  • Chargeable Weight: The higher of gross or volume weight
